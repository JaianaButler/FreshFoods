About


At FreshFoods, we strive for the most efficient and easy home delivery online shopping system. Not only are our products fresh and delicious, but they are healthy too! To top it all off, we have amazing recipes for you to try out at home! FreshFoods began way back in September of 2019, and it has come a long way since. We began as a start-up group project for Software Engineering at Georgia State University. Look how far we have come! We are still just a start-up group project! Now, that's called progress! Our FreshFoods team - Jaiana, Hiba, Ben, Wale, and John - envisioned an online shopping system that was user-friendly and efficient for online shoppers and voila! FreshFoods was born! We hope that you enjoy our recipes and the easy online shopping functionalities available on our website. Happy shopping!
